PAMULINAWEN FESTIVAL

Pamulinawen Festival celebrates the religious, patronal feast of St. William the Hermit and the cultural and historical heritage of the people in the city of Laoag. The month long calendar of pamulinawen festival features many public spectacles and community activities.There are cpncert, trade fair, grand parade, street dance, Calesa parade, sports, beauty pagents like Miss and Mister Laoag, Valentines Day program, pet show etc. On the other hand, religous activities are taken care of by St. William Catherdal.There are the nine-day novena and mass, fluvial procession, the pntificial mass on the day, etc.
