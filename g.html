LAOAG CITY,ILOCOS NORTE

Laoag City is popular known as the "Sunshine City" that has clear atmosphere with beautiful,blue skyline.
Its name is said to come from an Ilocano term, "LAWAG".The city is a vibrant dynamic growth center in the north,a truly unique destination offering surprises,panoramic sandunes,lush vegetation,scenic beaches,high land lures,historical and heritage sites.

It is the business and trading center of Ilocos Norte,boasting numerous diversified business activities and establishments.Hotel,restaurants,bars,banks and other establishment are located in the central business district.Of particular interes is the Laoag City Commercial Complex,where establishment offer Ilocano products such as Inabel,Bagnet,Longganisa,Sukang Iloko and other Ilocano products.

The most commonly used mode of transport when getting around Laoag are tricycles,jeepneys and mini-vans.Jeepneys have a fixed route,while tricycles can take you to ineer streets and destination points that are close to each other. Most popular tourist sites and landmarks around Laoag City like Singking Bell Tower,Museo Ilocos and St. William Cathedral are near each other an can be visited by foot.
